About this view — 549,096 Total Interpretations

This tab includes the 314,361 blank windows as additional True Negatives. Blank windows are sample–substance pairs where the instrument did not detect any chromatographic signal and were correctly left unflagged by the VA. Metrics that depend only on TP/FP/FN (Precision, Recall, F1) are unchanged. Metrics that use TN (Accuracy, Specificity, FPR, AUC) improve as errors dilute into a larger universe of correct classifications.
